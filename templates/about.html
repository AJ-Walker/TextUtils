About TextUtils.

TextUtils is a website were you can enter your text and the text analyzer will do some of the task for you. You can select what you want to do with your text from the below options, available were you enter your text.


These are the few options available for you :

Remove Punctuation Uppercase Lowercase NewLine Remover Extra Space Remover Character Count

Removes Punctuation from the text. If the Punctuation is from this list
'''!()-[]{};:'"\,<>./?@#$%^&*_~'''.

All of your text is Uppercased.

All of your text is Lowercased.

Newlines from your text are removed if it exists.

Extra spaces are removed from text if it exists.

Total Number of Characters are counted. You can count the character after doing some text utilization or before doing any text utilization.
